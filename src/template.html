TravelHunt

TravelHunt is an application with making the most of your trips in mind. It allows users to plan out points of interest anywhere they intend on traveling and, once there, view those points of interest on a map relative to their location. From there, a user can select a POI and be given a Google Maps route to get them there and check it off the list! Built Using: Angular 7, Firebase
